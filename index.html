Barn Owl

The Barn Owl (Tyto alba) is one of the most recognizable and widely distributed owls in the world. Known for its heart-shaped face, ghostly white appearance, and eerie screeching call, the barn owl plays a vital role in ecosystems by controlling rodent populations. It is found on every continent except Antarctica and thrives in open landscapes close to human settlements.

King Cobra

The King Cobra (Ophiophagus hannah) is one of the most iconic and formidable snakes in the world. Known for its impressive size, distinctive hood, and potent venom, the king cobra plays an important role in maintaining ecological balance by controlling populations of other snakes. It is the longest venomous snake on Earth and is capable of raising a large portion of its body when threatened. The species is native to forests and grasslands of South and Southeast Asia, where it thrives in warm, humid environments.

Mongoose

The Mongoose (family Herpestidae) is a small but agile mammal widely recognized for its quick reflexes and bold behavior. Known for its ability to confront venomous snakes, the mongoose plays an important ecological role by controlling insect, rodent, and reptile populations. It has a slender body, sharp claws, and keen senses that make it an effective hunter. Mongooses are found across Africa, Asia, and parts of southern Europe, thriving in habitats ranging from forests and grasslands to human settlements.

Axolotl

The Axolotl (Ambystoma mexicanum) is a unique and fascinating amphibian known for its ability to retain juvenile features throughout its life. Famous for its feathery external gills and remarkable regenerative abilities, the axolotl plays an important role in its freshwater ecosystem by preying on small aquatic organisms. Unlike most salamanders, it remains fully aquatic and does not undergo complete metamorphosis. The species is native to the lake systems of central Mexico, where it inhabits cool, slow-moving waters.

Armadilo

The Armadillo (family Dasypodidae) is a distinctive mammal best known for its protective, armor-like shell. Recognized for its strong digging abilities and nocturnal habits, the armadillo helps maintain soil health by controlling insect populations and aerating the ground. It has a long snout, sharp claws, and a body covered in bony plates that provide defense against predators. Armadillos are native to the Americas and are commonly found in grasslands, forests, and semi-arid regions.

Tamandua

The Southern Tamandua (Tamandua tetradactyla), also known as the lesser anteater, is a medium-sized mammal recognized for its long snout and powerful claws. Specialized for feeding on ants and termites, it plays an important role in controlling insect populations within its ecosystem. The southern tamandua has a prehensile tail and strong forelimbs that allow it to climb trees and tear open nests with ease. It is native to South America, where it inhabits forests, savannas, and grassland regions.

Sova Kukuvija

Sova kukuvija (Tyto alba) jedna je od najprepoznatljivijih i najrasprostranjenijih sova na svetu. Prepoznatljiva je po licu u obliku srca, bledom, gotovo belom perju i jezivom, prodornom kriku. Sova kukuvija ima važnu ulogu u ekosistemima jer kontroliše populacije glodara. Prisutan je na svim kontinentima osim Antarktika i najčešće nastanjuje otvorene predele u blizini ljudskih naselja.

Kraljevska Kobra

Kraljevska kobra (Ophiophagus hannah) jedna je od najpoznatijih i najopasnijih zmija na svetu. Poznata je po svojoj impresivnoj veličini, karakterističnoj kapuljači i snažnom otrovu, a ima važnu ulogu u održavanju ekološke ravnoteže kontrolišući populacije drugih zmija. To je najduža otrovnica na svetu i sposobna je da podigne veliki deo tela kada se oseća ugroženo. Prirodno je rasprostranjena u šumama i travnjacima Južne i Jugoistočne Azije.

Mungos

Mungos (porodica Herpestidae) je mali, ali veoma okretan sisar poznat po brzim refleksima i hrabrom ponašanju. Poznat je po sposobnosti da se suprotstavi otrovnim zmijama i ima važnu ekološku ulogu u kontroli populacija insekata, glodara i gmizavaca. Odlikuje ga vitko telo, oštre kandže i izuzetno razvijena čula. Mungosi su rasprostranjeni u Africi, Aziji i delovima južne Evrope.

Aksolotl

Aksolotl (Ambystoma mexicanum) je jedinstven i fascinantan vodozemac poznat po zadržavanju larvalnih osobina tokom celog života. Prepoznatljiv je po perjastim spoljašnjim škrgama i izuzetnoj sposobnosti regeneracije, a u svom vodenom ekosistemu ima važnu ulogu kao predator manjih organizama. Za razliku od većine daždevnjaka, ne prolazi potpunu metamorfozu i ostaje potpuno vodeni organizam. Poreklom je iz jezerskih sistema centralnog Meksika.

Armadilo

Armadilo (porodica Dasypodidae) je neobičan sisar najpoznatiji po svom zaštitnom oklopu nalik panciru. Poznat je po snažnim sposobnostima kopanja i noćnom načinu života, a doprinosi ekosistemu kontrolišući populacije insekata i poboljšavajući strukturu zemljišta. Ima izduženu njušku, jake kandže i telo prekriveno koštanim pločama. Armadili su poreklom iz Amerika i nastanjuju travnjake, šume i polusušne predele.

Tamandua

Južni tamandua (Tamandua tetradactyla), poznat i kao mali mravojed, srednje je veliki sisar prepoznatljiv po dugoj njušci i snažnim kandžama. Specijalizovan je za ishranu mravima i termitima, čime ima važnu ulogu u kontroli populacija insekata. Poseduje hvataljivi rep i snažne prednje udove koji mu omogućavaju penjanje i razbijanje gnezda. Prirodno je rasprostranjen u Južnoj Americi, gde naseljava šume, savane i travnata područja.
